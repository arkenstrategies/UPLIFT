Central Coast Talent Pipeline Tool

Where talent
meets opportunity
on the Central Coast.

Over the past year, through Uplift Central Coast, the region's California Jobs First initiative, REACH and the Economic Development Collaborative (EDC) have served as regional backbone organizations, convening workforce, community college, university, industry, and economic development partners across Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, and Ventura counties. Through this work, we heard directly from employers of all sizes in precision manufacturing and advanced business services.

The findings were consistent: the region already has many of the ingredients needed for success. The opportunity is to align those assets into a stronger, more connected regional talent pipeline.

This tool brings together what already exists and maps it alongside what employers are asking for, giving every partner in the region a shared picture to better align investments, strengthen workforce pathways, and respond to industry demand.
